CHICKEN CAESAR SALAD

INGREDIENT LIST

***FOR ONE SERVING***

  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1/2 bunch Romaine lettuce
  • 2 slices of wheat bread
  • Caesar salad dressing:
    • 4-5 tablespoons mayonnaise mixed with olive oil
    • 1/2 tablespoon of dijon mustard
    • 1/2 tablespoon of anchovy paste
    • Juice from 1/4 lemon
    • dash of Worchestershire sauce

PROCEDURES

PREPARATION

  • Set the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit; meanwhile, gently cut the sliced bread into 1/2 inch pieces and cut the Romaine lettuce into 1/2 inch pieces.
  • If the chicken is at room temperature, place it in the freezer for 15 minutes to make it easier to cut.
  • Cut the chicken breast against the grain into long, thin strips and season with salt, pepper, and the Caesar salad dressing.

COOKING

  1. Place the bread pieces into the oven once it's done preheating.
  2. Meanwhile, grill the chicken breast pieces.
  3. Once done, plunge them into ice water bath and submerge for 20 minutes; this immediately stops the cooking process & allows the chicken to firm up and stay tender.
  4. Toss the salad by combining the dressing, chicken breast pieces, Romaine lettuce, and the dresisng.
  5. ENJOY!

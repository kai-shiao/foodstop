BRAISED PORK

INGREDIENT LIST

***FOR ONE SERVING***

  • 1 lb pork hocks
  • Dark soy sauce
  • Rice wine
  • 5-spice powdered mix
  • Anise
  • Ground black pepper
  • A head of garlic

PROCEDURES

  1. Briefly rinse pork hocks and then pat dry.
  2. Mix dark soy sauce, rice wine, 5-spice powdered mix, anise, ground black pepper, a head of garlic with water until it covers the pork hocks by about 2 inches.
  3. Bring to a boil and then simmer for about 2 hours.
  4. Remove from pot and save the remaining liquid (now having a sauce-like consistency).

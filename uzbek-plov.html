UZBEK PLOV

INGREDIENT LIST

***FOR ONE SERVING***

  • 1/2 yellow onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 1/2 lb. Lamb chops
  • white(not brown) Basmati rice
  • Ground coriander
  • Ground cumin
  • Ground chili powder
  • Ground paprika
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1 head of garlic
  • OPTONAL: barberries, raisins

PROCEDURES

PREPARATION

  1. Briefly rinse lamb chops and pat dry using paper towels.
  2. Mix equal amounts of coriander, cumin, chili powder, paprika together and use it to season the lamb and reserve some for later use.
  3. Trim excess fat from lamb chops.(This prevents your dish from becoming too greasy.)
  4. Cut carrots into long thin strips of about 1/8 inch in width.
  5. Cut onions into thin cross sections of about the same width.

COOKING

  1. Cook chopped onions, carrots, and the seasoned lamb chops together for about 10-15 minutes on medium-high heat in a pot to bring out the flavour. (DO NOT ADD ANY COOKING OIL AS LAMB CONTAINS SIGNIFICANT FAT CONTENT.)
  2. While this is cooking, rinse about 1 cup of Basmati rice to remove excess starch and remove outer skin from the garlic head.
  3. After 10-15 minutes of cooking the lamb chops, carrots, and onions, add the Basmati rice, garlic head, & the remaining spice mix into the pot; then add salt and pepper
  4. Add water so that everything in the pot becomes only covered by the water by about 0.5 inch. Stir to ensure uniform distribution.
  5. Bring to a boil and then turn the heat down and simmer for about 20 minutes.
  6. ENJOY!
